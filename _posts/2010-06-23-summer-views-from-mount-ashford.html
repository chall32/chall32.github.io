Didn't you know Ashford had a 'mountain'?

OK, OK, Ashford doesn't.  However, it does have a triangulation point (aka a Trig point).  Just.  Where?  Here:



Yeah OK, it's actually on top of Collier's Hill located about halfway between Kingsnorth and Aldington, but hey, it's a 100% 'Kosher' trig point!

Having lived in Ashford for almost 10 years, and being that the trig point is only about a 15 minute cycle ride from home, it had to be worth a visit.  It didn't disappoint.
